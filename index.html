How It Works

Simple for employees,
powerful for admins

Four steps to complete equipment control across your CoExcel operation.

01

Sign Up & Login

Employees create an account with their ID and department. Admin approves access and assigns a role.

02

View the Dashboard

See all equipment at a glance — total inventory, status (available, in use, repair), and recent activity.

03

Add or Remove Equipment

Log equipment in when it arrives, or mark it as issued, removed, or under repair with one form.

04

Request New Equipment

Raise a purchase request for any item. Your manager gets notified and can approve or reject instantly.
